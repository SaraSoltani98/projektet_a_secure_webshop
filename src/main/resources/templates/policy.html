01 What information we collect

When you create an account or make a purchase, we collect the following personal data:

  • Email address — used for login and order communication
  • Password — stored encrypted using BCrypt, never in plain text
  • Consent — we record whether you have agreed to our terms

We only collect data that is strictly necessary. We do not sell your data to third parties.

02 How we use your information

  • Authenticating your login
  • Processing and managing your orders
  • Sending order confirmations
  • Complying with legal obligations

03 How we store your data

Passwords are hashed using BCrypt and are never readable by anyone. Session data is managed by Spring Security and expires on logout.

04 Your rights under GDPR

  • Right of access — request a copy of your data
  • Right to erasure — delete your account and data
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to portability — receive data in machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to how we process your data

You can exercise these rights from your profile page.

05 Cookies

We use essential cookies only. For full details see our Cookie information page.
